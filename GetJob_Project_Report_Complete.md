# CHAPTER 1: INTRODUCTION

## Overview of GetJob system  
The GetJob system is designed to provide an efficient way for job seekers to manage their job applications and track their progress. With numerous job openings available, it helps candidates avoid the chaos and confusion of managing applications manually.

## Background of job tracking challenges  
Job seekers often face challenges like missed deadlines, forgotten applications, and unclear application statuses. These issues lead to stress and anxiety about the job search process.

## Motivation for the project  
The motivation behind developing GetJob was to create a solution that alleviates these challenges and fosters a more organized and efficient job application process.

## Scope of the project  
The application focuses on job seekers, providing them with features such as application tracking, resume management, and job listing organization.

## Organization of the report  
This report is organized into chapters that detail each aspect of the project, from inception to testing and future improvements.


# CHAPTER 2: PROBLEM STATEMENT

## Background of job application tracking problems  
The traditional methods of job application tracking, such as spreadsheets or handwritten notes, can be ineffective and error-prone.

## Current challenges faced by job seekers  
Many job seekers struggle to keep track of multiple applications, leading to missed opportunities.

## Problem definition  
Current job application tracking methods are cumbersome and unreliable.

## Need for automated solution  
An automated solution provides ease of use, organization, and real-time updates on application statuses.
